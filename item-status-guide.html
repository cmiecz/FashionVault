How to Change Status

Universal steps for any status change

1

Open the Item

Tap any item in your closet to view its details.

2

Find the Status Section

Look for the status badge or label (usually near the top). It shows the current status.

3

Tap to Edit

Tap the status itself, or look for an edit/pencil icon next to it.

4

Select New Status

Choose from: Active, Lent, Donated, Sold, or Tossed.

5

Fill In Details

Depending on status, you'll see different fields to fill in (who you lent it to, sale price, donation value, etc.).

6

Add Notes (Optional)

Add context: "Donated to Goodwill downtown" or "Sold on Poshmark for $45".

7

Save Changes

Tap "Save" or "Update". The item moves to its new status category!

Returning Items

When they return the item:

  1. Open the item details
  2. Tap "Mark as Returned" button
  3. Check item condition
  4. Add return notes if needed
  5. Status automatically changes back to "Active"
