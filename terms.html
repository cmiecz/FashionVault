Household Sharing

Premium subscribers can enable household sharing to sync wardrobes with family members.

How Household Sharing Works

  • A household admin creates a household and invites members via invite code
  • All household members can view, add, edit, and delete shared wardrobe items
  • All members see all shared wardrobe data within the household
  • Real-time synchronization across all household members' devices

Household Privacy and Permissions

By enabling household sharing, you acknowledge that:

  • All household members will have access to shared wardrobe data, including photos and item details
  • Any household member can add, modify, or delete shared items
  • You are responsible for only inviting trusted individuals to your household
  • The household admin has the ability to remove members or delete the household

Leaving or Deleting Households

  • Any member can leave a household at any time through app settings
  • The household admin can delete the household at any time
  • When a household is deleted, shared wardrobe data is removed from all members' accounts
  • Individual members' personal (non-shared) wardrobe data is never affected by household deletion
