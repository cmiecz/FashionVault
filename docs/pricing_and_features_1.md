# Pricing, Premium Features & Credits

---

## Plan Overview

### Free Plan
- **Monthly AI Credits:** 10
- **Rollover:** No (resets to 10 each month)
- **Ads:** Yes
- **Premium Features:** No access

### Premium Plan
- **Monthly AI Credits:** 150
- **Rollover:** Yes (capped at 300)
- **Ads:** No (ad-free experience)
- **Premium Features:** Yes (full access)

---

## Core Features (No Credits Required)

These features are available to all users on both Free and Premium plans:

- **Wardrobe Management** – Browse closet and view item details
- **Manual Item Entry** – Add and edit items without AI assistance
- **Category Management** – Organize and manage custom categories
- **Store Management** – Set up and manage custom purchase stores
- **Shopping List** – Create and manage shopping lists
- **Maintenance Tracking** – Track item maintenance (where available)
- **Saved Outfits** – Save and assign outfit combinations
- **Credits Dashboard** – View balance and usage history

---

## AI-Powered Features (Credit-Based)

Each action consumes credits only after successful completion.

| Feature | Credits | Use |
|---------|--------:|-----|
| AI Item Scan | 1 | Scan item from photo to add to wardrobe |
| Bulk AI Scan | 1 per image | Batch scan multiple items (stops when credits run out) |
| Style Match | 1 | Analyze store item photo for style recommendations |
| AI Outfit Suggestion | 0.5 | Weekly planner outfit recommendations |
| Virtual Try-On | 1 | Apply clothing item to avatar |
| Virtual Try-On Avatar Creation | 20 | Create a new avatar profile |
| Avatar Variations | 60 | Generate 3 variations of your avatar |
| Photo Background Removal | 1 | Remove background from product photo |
| Generate Product Photo | 1 | Create a new product photo |

## Premium-Only Features

Access these premium-exclusive features with a Premium subscription:

- **Ad-Free Experience** – No ads while using the app
- **Household Sharing** – Share wardrobe within your household
- **Cross-Household Sharing** – Share items with other households
- **Usage & Analytics** – View detailed usage statistics and insights
- **Business Mode** – Enhanced tools for professional/business use
- **Storage Locations with QR Labels** – Track where items are stored with QR code labels

---

## Credits System

### How Credits Work
- Credits are **checked before** any AI action
- Credits are **spent only after success** (failed operations don't cost credits)
- Free users receive **10 credits monthly** with no rollover
- Premium users receive **150 credits monthly** with rollover (max 300 total)

### Purchasing Credits
- Credit pack purchase UI exists
- Real payment integration is simulated (not yet wired)

## Important Notes & Known Gaps

### Copy vs. Behavior Discrepancies
| Issue | Current Status |
|-------|---|
| Free plan item limit | "Limited to 5 items" is not enforced in code |
| Credit expiration | "Credits never expire" conflicts with free monthly reset behavior |
| Storage locations | Mostly gated in UI but not fully enforced |

### Reset Schedule
- **Free Plan:** 10 credits reset to 10 every month (no accumulation)
- **Premium Plan:** 150 credits added monthly, rolls over up to 300 maximum

---

## Summary

**Free Plan** is ideal for casual users who want manual wardrobe management without AI assistance. Limited monthly AI credits provide a taste of premium features.

**Premium Plan** unlocks the full AI experience with generous monthly credits, household sharing, and ad-free browsing—perfect for power users and families managing shared wardrobes.
