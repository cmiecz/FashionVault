Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Introduction

ShepRidge LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates Fashion Vault ("the Service"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information with care and transparency. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

By using Fashion Vault, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Service.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect personal information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name and email address
  • Account credentials and authentication data
  • Apple ID association (for subscription management)
  • Profile information and preferences
  • Payment information (processed securely through Apple's In-App Purchase system and RevenueCat)

Wardrobe Data

We collect and store comprehensive information about your wardrobe, including:

  • Photos of clothing items, shoes, and accessories
  • Item details: brand, size, color, style, category
  • Storage location information (where items are kept)
  • Purchase information: date, store, price, receipts
  • Custom tags and categories you create
  • Outfit combinations and lookbooks
  • Calendar and outfit planning data

AI-Generated Data

When you use our Premium AI features, we collect and generate:

  • AI-generated item descriptions from scanning
  • Virtual try-on images (generated temporarily during sessions)
  • Digital twin profile images (your virtual avatar)
  • AI analysis results and recommendations

Credit and Transaction Data

For Premium subscribers, we track:

  • Credit balance and monthly allocation
  • Credit purchase history
  • Credit usage logs (which features used, when, and how many credits)
  • Subscription status and billing information

Household Sharing Data

If you enable household sharing, we collect:

  • Household membership information
  • Household invite codes
  • Shared wardrobe data accessible to household members
  • Household member permissions and roles

Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information about your device and how you use our app:

  • Device information: model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • App usage statistics: features used, time spent, interactions
  • Location data (only with your permission, for store reminder features)
  • Crash reports and error logs
  • Performance and diagnostic data

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

Core Service Functionality

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our digital wardrobe services
  • Store and organize your wardrobe data
  • Enable outfit planning and calendar features
  • Synchronize data across your devices
  • Enable household wardrobe sharing and syncing

AI Features and Processing

  • Process photos for AI item scanning and auto-fill features
  • Generate virtual try-on visualizations
  • Create and maintain digital twin profiles
  • Provide AI-powered style suggestions and outfit recommendations
  • Improve AI accuracy and model performance (using aggregated, anonymized data)

Account and Subscription Management

  • Process transactions and manage subscriptions via RevenueCat and Apple
  • Track credit balance and usage
  • Manage account settings and preferences
  • Authenticate and secure your account

Communication and Support

  • Send updates, notifications, and service announcements
  • Respond to support requests and communications
  • Notify you about subscription renewals or changes

Analytics and Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Monitor app performance and fix bugs
  • Conduct research and development for new features

Security and Compliance

  • Detect, prevent, and address fraud or abuse
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Comply with legal obligations and respond to legal requests

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate Fashion Vault. Each service has its own privacy policy governing how they handle your data:

Infrastructure and Backend Services

  • Supabase: Database, authentication, and storage infrastructure (US East Coast region). Stores all user data, wardrobe information, and photos. Privacy policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

Payment and Subscription Services

  • Apple In-App Purchase: Processes all subscription payments and manages billing. Privacy policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management and payment processing platform. Privacy policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

AI Services

  • OpenAI GPT-4 Vision: Powers AI item scanning feature. Photos are temporarily sent to OpenAI for processing and then deleted. Privacy policy: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy
  • AI Image Generation Services: Powers virtual try-on and digital twin creation features. Images are processed and deleted after generation. Specific service details available upon request.

Analytics and Performance

  • Apple Analytics: Provides crash reporting and usage analytics (if you have enabled this in your device settings)

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We share your information only in the following circumstances:

What We DO NOT Do

  • Sell your personal data or photos to advertisers or data brokers
  • Share your wardrobe data outside of your household
  • Use your photos for marketing purposes without explicit consent
  • Share personally identifiable information with AI training datasets

What We DO Share

  • Apple: Transaction data and subscription information (required for App Store functionality)
  • RevenueCat: Subscription status and payment processing data
  • Supabase: All user data, as our backend infrastructure provider
  • AI Services (OpenAI and image generation providers): Photos you submit for AI processing (temporarily, then deleted after processing)
  • Household Members: Shared wardrobe data when you enable household sharing (all household members can view shared items)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with notice to users)

AI Data Processing

When you use AI features, photos are temporarily sent to third-party AI services (OpenAI for scanning, AI image generation services for try-on and digital twins). These services process your photos and return results, then delete the images. We do not authorize these services to use your photos for training AI models.

Data Storage and Security

Where Your Data is Stored

Your data is stored using the following infrastructure:

  • Database: Supabase (PostgreSQL database)
  • Region: United States (East Coast)
  • Image Storage: Supabase storage buckets
  • Backup: Automated backups retained for 30 days

Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses HTTPS/SSL encryption
  • Encryption at Rest: Your data is encrypted when stored in our database and storage systems
  • Row-Level Security (RLS): Database-level security policies ensure users can only access their own data
  • Authentication: Secure authentication via Supabase with industry-standard protocols
  • Access Controls: Strict internal access controls and monitoring
  • Regular Audits: Ongoing security reviews and updates

Data Retention

We retain your data according to the following policies:

  • Active Account Data: Stored while your account is active
  • Deleted Items: Soft-deleted (marked as deleted but retained for 30 days for recovery)
  • Account Deletion: When you delete your account, your data is permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Backup Retention: Automated backups retained for 30 days
  • AI-Processed Images: Temporary photos sent to AI services are deleted immediately after processing unless you save the results

Security Limitations

While we implement robust security measures, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Access and Control

  • Access: View and access all your personal information and wardrobe data through the app
  • Update: Modify or correct your personal information through app settings
  • Delete: Delete individual wardrobe items, photos, or your entire account
  • Export: Request a copy of your wardrobe data in a portable format

Privacy Controls

  • Opt-Out of Analytics: Disable crash reporting and usage analytics through your device settings
  • Location Services: Control location access for store reminder features in device settings
  • Household Sharing: Leave household or disable sharing at any time

Subscription Management

  • Manage Subscription: View, modify, or cancel your subscription through Apple ID Account Settings
  • View Credits: Check your credit balance and usage history in the app

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, you can:

  • Use the privacy controls within the app settings
  • Contact us at support@fashionvault.app
  • Submit a request through our support page

We will respond to your request within 30 days.

AI Features and Privacy

Our AI features involve special data processing that you should understand:

How AI Processing Works

  • AI Item Scanning: When you scan an item, the photo is sent to OpenAI's GPT-4 Vision API. OpenAI processes the image, extracts item details, and returns the results. The image is then deleted from OpenAI's servers.
  • Virtual Try-On: Your digital twin and clothing item photos are sent to AI image generation services. The service generates a visualization and returns it. Images are deleted after processing.
  • Digital Twin Creation: Your photo is processed by AI services to create a virtual profile. The original photo is deleted after the digital twin is generated.

AI Data Protection

  • Photos submitted to AI services are processed temporarily and then deleted
  • AI services do not use your photos to train their models (per our agreements)
  • AI-generated results (scans, try-ons, digital twins) are stored in your account only if you save them
  • You can delete any AI-generated content at any time

Your AI Privacy Choices

  • AI features are optional - you can use Fashion Vault without them
  • You control when to use AI features (each use consumes credits)
  • You can delete your digital twin profile at any time
  • AI-generated content belongs to you and can be deleted

Children's Privacy

Fashion Vault is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at support@fashionvault.app. We will take steps to delete such information from our systems.

Users between the ages of 13 and 18 must have parental or guardian permission to use the Service.
