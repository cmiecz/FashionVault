MODE 1

Weekly Planner

Plan your entire week with AI-generated outfit suggestions

1

Access Weekly Planner

Tap the "Stylist" tab in the bottom navigation, then make sure you're in "Weekly" mode at the top of the screen.

You'll see a weekly calendar view with weather forecasts for each day!

2

Add an Outfit for a Day

Tap on any day in the calendar, then tap "Add Outfit" or the "+" button.

MODE 2

Try On Mode

Generate outfit suggestions and visualize them on your digital twin

Note: This mode requires a virtual try-on profile. Create one here

1

Switch to Try On Mode

Tap the "Try On" tab at the top of the Stylist screen.

2

Generate Outfit Suggestions

Tap "Generate Outfits" to create 10 AI-styled outfit combinations from your closet.

The AI creates complete looks with coordinated tops, bottoms, shoes, and accessories!

3

Browse Outfits

Scroll through the outfit cards. Each card shows:

    All items in the outfit

  • image

  • visibility

5

Save Favorites

Love an outfit? Tap "Save Outfit" to add it to your collection. You can optionally assign it to a specific day or occasion.

Troubleshooting & FAQ

Common questions and solutions

Credit costs vary by feature. Check your balance display for specific costs. Weekly planning and try-on features will show credit requirements before generating.

Make sure your items are properly tagged with accurate occasions, styles, and colors. Also, use the occasion filter to specify what type of outfit you need. The AI learns from your saved outfits over time!

Our AI follows professional styling rules, but personal taste varies! Simply generate new suggestions or manually edit the outfit. If you consistently dislike certain combinations, make sure items are tagged with compatible style tags.

Make sure you've accepted sharing invitations from other household members. Go to Settings → Household & Sharing to check your shared closets. Refresh the Stylist screen after accepting invites.

Check that you've granted location permissions to the app in your device settings. The app needs your location to fetch accurate weather data. If you recently changed locations, pull down to refresh the calendar.
