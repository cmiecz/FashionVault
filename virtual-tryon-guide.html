Detailed Step-by-Step Guide

Everything you need to know about virtual try-on

1

Access Virtual Try-On

Tap the "AI Try On" tab in the bottom navigation bar.

You'll see your closet items organized and ready to try on!

2

Browse Your Closet

Scroll through your items to find what you want to try on. Items are displayed with clear photos, names, and categories.

You can try on multiple items together to create complete outfits!

3

Select Items to Try On

Tap items to add them to your try-on selection. Selected items will show a checkmark or highlight.

Tops

Shirts, blouses, sweaters

Bottoms

Pants, skirts, shorts

Dresses

One-piece outfits

Shoes

Sneakers, heels, boots

Jackets

Outerwear

Accessories

Shown separately

Pro Tip: Try on complete outfits (2-5 items) for the best results!

4

Review Your Selection

Check the selected items preview at the bottom or side of the screen. You can:

  • add

    Add more items by tapping

  • remove

    Remove items by tapping again

  • clear_all

    Clear all to start fresh

5

Check Your Profile

Before generating, the app checks if you have a virtual try-on profile.

check_circle

If you have a profile

You're all set! Continue to next step.

warning

If you don't have one

Tap "Create Profile" (takes 3-5 minutes)

6

Generate Virtual Try-On

Tap the "Generate Virtual Try-On" button. You'll see:

paid

Credit cost confirmation (2 credits per item)

hourglass_empty

Processing indicator

schedule

Estimated time (20-30 seconds)

The AI is creating a realistic image of you wearing these items!

7

View Your Result

Once generation completes, you'll see the outfit on your digital twin! The result shows:

face

Your face and body proportions

checkroom

Selected clothing fitted to your body

palette

Accurate colors and styling

auto_awesome

How the complete outfit looks together

Bags and large accessories may appear below the main image for clarity.

8

Save or Regenerate

Now you can:

bookmark

Add to your collection and assign to a day/occasion

autorenew

Regenerate

Create a new version with same items (may look slightly different)

sync_alt

Try Different Items

Go back and select different combinations to compare

Troubleshooting & FAQ

Common questions and solutions

Virtual try-on requires a profile with your photo and body settings. Tap the message or go to Settings → Virtual Try-On Profile → Create Profile. It only takes 3-5 minutes! See our Profile Creation Guide.

It costs 2 credits per item. A 3-item outfit (top, bottom, shoes) costs 6 credits. Your credit balance appears at the top of the screen. Tap it to purchase more credits if needed.

Try these solutions: Check your internet connection, make sure you have enough credits, reduce the number of items (try 2-3 items first), verify your profile is complete (go to Settings), or try again as temporary server issues happen occasionally.

This can happen for several reasons: Your profile photo quality (update with better selfie), item photo quality (re-photograph with better lighting), too many items at once (try fewer items), complex patterns (AI may simplify designs), or try regenerating as results can vary slightly.

Follow these best practices: Update your profile with high-quality, front-facing photo; use AI scan when adding items for professional photos; select 2-4 items rather than too many; ensure accurate body settings in your profile; regenerate your digital twin if your appearance has changed.

Small accessories may not show clearly in try-on results. The feature works best with clothing items (tops, bottoms, dresses, shoes, outerwear). Bags and large accessories are shown separately below the main result.

Typically 20-30 seconds. Factors that affect speed: Number of items (more items = longer processing), AI model availability (peak times may be slightly slower), image processing requirements, and your internet connection speed.

Instant loading means you're viewing a cached result! If you've tried that exact combination in the last 30 days, it loads from cache with no wait and no credit cost. New combinations require AI generation.

Your try-on uses the photo from your profile. Update your profile photo in Settings → Virtual Try-On Profile → Edit Profile. After updating, regenerate your digital twin, and future try-ons will use your new look.

Yes! Tap "Save Outfit" after viewing a try-on. Saved outfits appear in your Stylist → Saved Try-Ons section. You can also assign them to specific days or occasions.
