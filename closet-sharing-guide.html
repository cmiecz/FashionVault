Share items with friends, family, and neighbors! Cross-household closet sharing lets you collaborate on wardrobes, lend tools and equipment, and plan outfits together—all while maintaining privacy and control over your items.

Understanding the Two-Code System

Your household has two different codes for security and flexibility

warning

Critical: Understand the Difference!

Using the wrong code gives unintended access. Read this section carefully to protect your privacy.

home

Full Access

Household Join Code

BLUE CODE

What it's for:

Adding family members to your SAME household

What it does:

  • • Gives full access to entire closet
  • • Syncs across all devices
  • • Shares everything automatically
  • • Creates one unified household

When to use it:

  • • Spouse or partner
  • • Roommates sharing a closet
  • • Children in your home
  • • Anyone you fully trust

⚠️ Security: Only share with people who should have access to ALL your items!

link

Selective Sharing

Connection Code

GREEN CODE

What it's for:

Connecting with DIFFERENT households for selective sharing

What it does:

  • • Share specific items only
  • • Nothing shared automatically
  • • Keeps households separate
  • • You control what they see

When to use it:

  • • Friends you lend clothes to
  • • Neighbors sharing tools
  • • Extended family
  • • Anyone outside your household

✓ Security: Safe to share with trusted friends!

Why Two Codes?

security

Security

tune

Share some things without sharing everything. Your neighbor doesn't need to see your entire wardrobe!

Connecting with Another Household

One-time setup before you can share items

1

Go to Settings

Open the app and tap Settings from the main menu or bottom navigation.

2

Find Household & Sharing

Scroll down to the "Household & Sharing" section. You'll see your household name and codes.

3

Get Your Connection Code

Find your Connection Code (Green). This is what you'll share with others.

smartphone

Show screen

content_copy

Copy code

mail

Send via email

edit

Write it down

4

Connect with Another Household

Tap "Connect with Another Household" or "Add Connection". A modal appears asking for their connection code.

5

Enter Their Connection Code

Type or paste the 6-character connection code they gave you.

✓ Make sure it's the green Connection Code, not the blue Join Code!

6

Confirm Connection

Tap "Connect" or "Confirm". The connection is established immediately!

celebration

Connected! You can now share items with each other.

No items are shared automatically - you choose what to share!
