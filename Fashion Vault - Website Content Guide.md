## FAQ

### How does the free tier work?
You get 10 AI credits per month to try features like scanning and outfit suggestions. Manual wardrobe management is unlimited.

### What happens when I run out of credits?
You can upgrade to Premium (150 credits/month + rollover) or purchase credit packs. Manual features still work without credits.

### Can I try Premium before paying?
Yes! We often offer trial periods. Check the app for current promotions.

### Does it work offline?
You can browse your wardrobe offline. AI features require internet connection.

### Can I export my data?
Yes! Your data is always yours. Export anytime from settings.

### How accurate is the AI scanning?
Very accurate for mainstream brands and common items. You can always edit details after scanning.

### Is my wardrobe data private?
Yes. We never sell your data. Household sharing is encrypted and only visible to people you invite.

### What if I cancel Premium?
You keep all your data and photos. Premium features lock, but you can still browse and manually manage items.

### Do credits expire?
- Free: Credits reset monthly (no rollover)
- Premium: Credits roll over up to 300 maximum

### Can I share with friends outside my household?
Yes! Premium includes cross-household sharing for lending items with tracking.
