How to Request to Borrow

For borrowers: asking permission

1

Find the Item

Browse items in a connected household's shared closet. Look for something they've shared with you.

2

Open Item Details

Tap the item to view its full details and photos.

3

Tap "Request to Borrow"

Look for the button (usually near bottom or in menu). A request form appears.

Note: If you don't see this button, the item might not be available for lending, or you're not connected to that household.

4

Fill In Request Details

  • When do you need it? Start and end dates
  • Why do you need it? "For my cousin's wedding" or "Weekend camping trip"
  • Pickup method: Will you pick it up, or should they drop it off?
  • Notes: Any special requests or information
5

Submit Request

Tap "Send Request". The owner receives a notification immediately!

6

Wait for Response

The request status shows "Pending". You'll get a notification when the owner responds.

Once the borrower picks up the item, either person can mark it as "Picked Up":

Step 1: Open the Request

Go to the borrow request details (both owner and borrower can access this).

Step 2: Tap "Mark as Picked Up"

Status changes from "Approved" to "Borrowed".

Step 3: Document Condition (Optional)

Take a photo, note any existing wear or damage. Protects both parties!

Step 4: Confirm Pickup Date

Date and time are automatically recorded.
