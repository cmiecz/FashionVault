Detailed Step-by-Step Guide

Everything you need to know about creating your profile

1

Access Profile Creation

Open your app and navigate to Settings. Look for the "Virtual Try-On Profile" section and tap the "Create Profile" button.

You'll see a welcome screen explaining the benefits of creating your profile. Tap "Get Started" when you're ready!

2

Upload Your Headshot Photo

Now it's time to add your photo! You have two options:

photo_camera

Take Photo

Use your camera to take a new selfie

photo_library

Choose from Gallery

Select an existing photo

lightbulb

Important:

Use a clear, front-facing selfie for the best results. Make sure your face is well-lit and fully visible.

3

AI Enhances Your Photo

Once you upload your photo, our AI automatically creates a professional headshot version. This happens in just a few seconds!

You'll see a preview of your AI-enhanced headshot. If you're happy with it, tap "Continue" to move forward. Not satisfied? You can regenerate it or upload a different photo.

âœ¨ Your features are preserved while creating a clean, professional version!

4

Set Your Body Preferences

Now we need some information to create an accurate digital twin:

wc Gender Presentation

Choose how you'd like your avatar styled:

  • Feminine
  • Masculine
  • Androgynous

accessibility Body Type

Select the body type that best matches yours. We offer 8 different options for each gender presentation to ensure accurate fit visualization.

height Height

Use the slider to set your height, or tap one of the preset buttons for quick selection (4'0" to 7'0").

All of this information helps us create a realistic digital twin that accurately shows how clothes will fit on you!

5

Generate Your Digital Twin

This is where the magic happens! Our AI will create a full-body digital twin based on your headshot and body preferences.

The generation process takes about 20-30 seconds. You'll see a loading screen with a progress indicator.

hourglass_empty

Generation in progress...

Creating your personalized digital twin

6

Choose Your Body Variation

Once generation is complete, you'll see 3 variations of your digital twin:

person_remove

Smaller Build

Slightly slimmer proportions

person

Standard Build

Matches your selection exactly

person_add

Larger Build

Slightly fuller proportions

Pick the one that looks most like you! This ensures the most realistic try-on results.

7

All Done!

Congratulations! Your virtual try-on profile is ready. Tap "Start Try-Ons" to begin trying on clothes from your closet, or return to Settings.

Your profile is automatically saved and synced to the cloud, so it's available on all your devices!

Managing Your Profile

View, edit, or delete your profile anytime

visibility

View Your Profile

Want to see your profile details? Go to Settings and tap on your profile card in the "Virtual Try-On Profile" section.

You'll see your digital twin, gender presentation, body type, and height.

edit

Edit Your Profile

Need to update your profile?

  1. Go to Settings
  2. Tap on your existing profile
  3. Tap the "Edit" button
  4. Make your changes
  5. Regenerate if needed

Note: Regenerating requires credits

delete

Delete Your Profile

If you want to start fresh:

  1. Go to Settings
  2. Tap on your profile
  3. Tap "Delete Profile"
  4. Confirm the deletion

This removes all profile data from your device and the cloud.
